Policy Overview

Last Updated: 13 Sep 2025

Our Commitment: SNEK has zero tolerance for any activity that endangers children. We prohibit Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, solicitation, and any exploitation of minors. Violations result in immediate enforcement actions and reports to the appropriate authorities.

1. Age Restriction

  • SNEK is strictly for adults aged 18 years and above.
  • Users must verify age during account creation. Accounts reasonably believed to be created by a minor are immediately suspended.
  • Appeals require adequate proof of age; unresolved cases are permanently removed.

2. Zero Tolerance for CSAM

  • We prohibit uploading, sharing, linking to, or soliciting any CSAM or sexualized content involving minors.
  • Confirmed CSAM leads to instant account termination and reports to law enforcement and relevant hotlines (e.g., national cybercrime portals).
  • We preserve necessary data and cooperate with investigations as required by law.

3. Prohibition of Grooming & Exploitation

  • Any attempt to solicit or groom minors, or to arrange sexual encounters, is strictly forbidden.
  • We also prohibit instructions that facilitate exploitation or trafficking of minors.
  • Such behavior results in a permanent ban and reports to authorities.

4. Reporting Mechanisms

  • Use in‑app tools to Report profiles, messages, or posts. Look for the Report menu.
  • Email urgent concerns to support@snek.co.in with screenshots, usernames, and timestamps.
  • For immediate danger, contact local emergency services (112 in India) and submit a complaint at cybercrime.gov.in.

5. Detection & Enforcement

  • We review reports, apply safety classifiers, and enforce policy consistently.
  • Actions may include warnings, feature restrictions, suspensions, and terminations.
  • We may preserve data related to violations for a time period required by law.

6. Cooperation with Authorities

  • We cooperate with lawful requests from child protection and law enforcement agencies.
  • We escalate credible threats and CSAM reports without prior notice to the user.
  • Disclosures are limited to what is legally required and proportionate.

7. Data Handling for Safety Reports

  • We process safety reports to investigate and prevent harm, consistent with our Privacy Policy.
  • Information provided in reports (e.g., screenshots, messages, IDs) may be retained for compliance obligations.

8. User Education & Awareness

  • SNEK periodically informs users that the platform is for adults only.
  • We encourage reporting of any suspected child endangerment to us and to local authorities.

9. Safety Tips

  • Do not share personal contact details with unknown users. Keep conversations within the app.
  • Report anyone asking for explicit images, money, or off‑platform contact, especially involving minors.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels unsafe, report and block.

10. Helpful Resources

  • India (Emergency): Dial 112. Report online crimes at cybercrime.gov.in.
  • India (Childline): Dial 1098 for child protection assistance.
  • Global: Contact local law enforcement or recognized child protection hotlines in your country.

11. Continuous Review

Our Trust & Safety team regularly updates this policy to align with evolving laws and platform policies (e.g., app store guidelines). Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

If you suspect a minor is at risk: report it in‑app, email support@snek.co.in, and contact local authorities immediately.

Nothing in this page limits SNEK’s right to act to protect users, comply with laws, or enforce our Terms.

Child Safety & Protection

Your safety is our top priority. Learn about our commitment to protecting minors and maintaining a safe community.

Age Verification

SNEK is exclusively for college students aged 18 and above.

Mandatory Age Verification: All users must provide their date of birth during registration. This information is validated against their college ID card and cannot be changed after initial setup.

College ID Verification: Every user must upload a valid college ID card which is manually reviewed by our team before account activation. This ensures only legitimate college students can access the platform.

Zero Tolerance for Minors: Any user found to be under 18 will have their account immediately terminated and permanently banned from the platform.

Content Moderation

We actively monitor and moderate all content shared on our platform.

  • Photo Verification: All profile photos are manually reviewed before approval to ensure appropriateness.
  • Real-time Monitoring: Our AI and human moderators work together to detect and remove inappropriate content in feeds, confessions, and chats.
  • Prohibited Content: We strictly prohibit nudity, sexual content, violence, hate speech, harassment, and any content that exploits or endangers minors.
  • Automated Detection: Our systems use advanced technology to detect and flag potentially harmful content before it reaches users.

User Safety Tools

We provide comprehensive tools to help you stay safe and in control.

Block & Report

Instantly block any user who makes you uncomfortable. Report inappropriate behavior, and our team will investigate within 24 hours.

Privacy Controls

Control who can see your profile and interact with you through comprehensive filter settings.

Clear Chat History

Delete your chat history at any time to maintain your privacy and digital wellbeing.

Deactivate Account

Take a break whenever you need. Your account can be deactivated and reactivated at your convenience.

Reporting & Response

We take every report seriously and act swiftly to protect our community.

How to Report: Use the report button available on profiles, posts, and chats. Provide as much detail as possible about the violation.

Our Response Time: All reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Emergency situations involving threats or illegal content are prioritized and reviewed immediately.

Consequences for Violators: Depending on severity, violators may receive warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans. We also cooperate with law enforcement when necessary.

Anonymous Reporting: Your identity is protected when you report content or users. The reported party will not know who filed the report.

Strictly Prohibited

The following activities will result in immediate account termination.

  • Impersonating another person or using fake credentials
  • Sharing or soliciting sexual content or nude images
  • Harassing, bullying, or threatening other users
  • Promoting illegal activities or substances
  • Soliciting money or financial information
  • Spamming or commercial advertising without permission
  • Attempting to access another user's account
  • Sharing personal information of others without consent
  • Using the platform if you are under 18 years of age

Report Safety Concerns

Help us maintain a safe community by reporting inappropriate behavior or content.

Emergency Situations

If you are experiencing an immediate threat to your safety or someone else's safety, please contact local law enforcement immediately.

For urgent safety concerns on SNEK, email safety@snek.app (reviewed within 1 hour)

Your Privacy

Your identity is protected when you file a report. The reported party will not know who submitted the report.

All reports are reviewed by our trained moderation team.

What You Can Report

  • • Inappropriate user behavior
  • • Harassing messages
  • • Fake or spam profiles
  • • Inappropriate posts (Feed)
  • • Offensive confessions
  • • Group misconduct
  • • Underage users
  • • Safety concerns

What Happens Next?

1
Review: Our moderation team reviews your report within 24 hours (urgent cases within 1 hour)
2
Investigation: We investigate the reported content or behavior thoroughly
3

4
Follow-up: We'll email you about the outcome if you provided your email address

1. Getting Started

Registration & Login

Step 1: Download the App

Download SNEK from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

Step 2: Choose Login Method

Sign up using Google authentication or email verification via OTP.

  • Google Login: Quick one-tap authentication
  • Email OTP: Receive a one-time password to your email

Step 3: Provide Your Name

Important: Your name must perfectly match the name on your college identity card. You cannot change this later.

Step 4: Enter Date of Birth

Important: Make sure your age is correct. You must be 18+ to use SNEK. This cannot be changed later.

Step 5: Select Gender

Choose how you identify yourself: Male or Female.

Animation: After completing these steps, you'll see a welcome animation before continuing.

2. Profile Setup

Step 6: Select Your State

Choose the state where your college is located. This helps us show you relevant connections.

Step 7: Select Your College

You'll only see colleges from the state you selected. Choose your current college from the list.

Example: If you selected "West Bengal", only colleges in West Bengal will appear.

Step 8: Upload College ID Card

Upload a clear photo of your college ID card. This is used for verification purposes.

  • ‚úì Ensure the ID is clearly visible
  • ‚úì Your name must match the name you provided
  • ‚úì Photo should be recent and not blurry

Step 9: Verification Selfie

Take a selfie for verification. This must match the face on your college ID card.

This selfie is only for verification and won't be shown on your profile.

Step 10: Add Profile Photos

Upload 2-6 clear photos of yourself for your profile.

  • Minimum: 2 photos required
  • Maximum: 6 photos allowed
  • Tip: Profiles with clear pics get verified earlier!

Animation: After uploading photos, you'll see an inspiring animation with quotes about images!

Step 11: Select Hobbies

Choose your hobbies from categorized options. Select at least 4 categories.

Categories may include: Sports, Arts, Music, Technology, and more.

Step 12: Subscription Introduction (Optional)

Learn about SNEK Premium features. You can:

  • Tap "Subscribe Now" to view premium plans
  • Tap "Skip" (top right corner) to continue with free version

‚úì Profile Complete! You'll see a completion animation with motivational quotes. Your profile will be verified within 1 hour.

3. Home & Matching

Note: Your profile must be verified before you can fully interact. Unverified profiles will see a verification pending message on all pages except the profile page.

Home Page Layout

The home page is designed similar to Happn with the following features:

Top Left: Profile Icon

Tap to go to your profile page

Top Right: Notifications

Opens from top to bottom

Top Right: Filter

Opens from bottom to top

Center: Profile Cards

Swipe right (like) or left (pass)

Profile Card Display

  • Name: Shown in bold letters
  • College Name: Shown below name in smaller letters
  • Note: No age display, no superlike, no boost buttons

How to Swipe

Swipe Right / Tap ‚ù§Ô∏è

You're interested in this person

  • If they also swipe right = Match!
  • They'll get a notification (unless they disabled it)
  • Both receive a match notification

Swipe Left / Tap ‚úó

Pass on this profile

  • They won't be notified
  • You might see them again later

Filter Options

1. College-wise Filter

Show only students from your college. Perfect for finding people you might meet on campus.

2. State-wise Filter

Show students from all colleges in your state. Great for meeting people from nearby colleges.

3. Don't Notify on Right Swipe

When enabled, others won't get a notification when you swipe right on them. They'll only know if you match.

Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:

  • ‚úì New matches
  • ‚úì Someone swiped right on you (tap to see their profile)
  • ‚úì New group created in your college
  • ‚úì Group event reminders (2 hours before)
  • ‚úì Profile verification status
  • ‚úì Note: Chat notifications appear in chat, not in notification center

4. Chat Feature

SNEK's chat interface is designed similar to Happn for a familiar and intuitive experience.

When Chats Are Created

A chat conversation is automatically created when two users match (both swipe right on each other).

Both users receive a notification to start the conversation.

Chat Features

  • Text Messages: Send and receive text messages in real-time
  • Image Sharing: Share photos within the chat
  • Read Receipts: See when messages are delivered and read

Chat Options

Clear Chat

Delete all messages in this conversation. The chat will remain but history is cleared.

Block User

Prevent this user from contacting you. They won't be notified.

Report User

Report inappropriate behavior or content to our moderation team.

Safety Tip: Never share personal information like phone numbers, addresses, or financial details in chat. If someone makes you uncomfortable, block and report them immediately.

5. Groups Feature

Groups help you meet people from your college who share your interests for activities like sports, music, hiking, and more.

Creating a Group (Premium Users Only)

Step 1: Premium Subscription Required

Only SNEK Premium subscribers can create groups.

  • Premium includes 5 free groups per month
  • Additional groups require a small fee

Step 2: Fill Group Details

  • Title: Name of the group/activity
  • Description: What the group is about
  • Spot: Meeting location on campus
  • Date & Time: When the activity will happen

Groups can be created up to 30 days in advance.

Step 3: Group is Created

All students from your college receive a notification about the new group.

Joining a Group

Premium Users

Join any group from their college for free

Free Users

Pay a small participation fee to join groups

Group Visibility

Before joining, you can see:

  • ‚úì Title
  • ‚úì Description

After joining, you can see:

  • ‚úì Spot (meeting location)
  • ‚úì Date and time
  • ‚úì Other group members
  • ‚úì Access to group chat

Group Chat

  • Group chat is created automatically when the group is created
  • All members can chat with each other
  • View other members' profiles by tapping their name
  • Chat is disabled 1 hour after the scheduled event time
  • Everyone receives a reminder notification 2 hours before the event

7. Confessions

Confessions is a semi-anonymous space for text-based posts visible only to students from your college.

Key Differences from Feed

  • ‚úì Text-only posts (no images or videos)
  • ‚úì Only visible to students from your college
  • ‚úì More private and community-focused

Creating a Confession

Write your text-based confession. Only students from your college will see it.

Your name and college are still displayed, but the audience is limited to your college community.

Interaction Options

  • Like: Show support for confessions
  • Comment: Share your perspective
  • Report: Flag inappropriate content

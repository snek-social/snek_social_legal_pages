Everything You Need to Connect

SNEK brings together the best features to help college students meet, connect, and build their community.

Match & Connect

Swipe through profiles of students from your college or state. Match with people who share your interests and start meaningful conversations.

Private Chats

Chat one-on-one with your matches. Share images, have real conversations, and build genuine connections with fellow students.

Interest-Based Groups

Join or create groups for activities like sports, music, hiking, and more. Meet people from your college who share your passions.

Social Feed

Share your moments, thoughts, and experiences with the entire SNEK community. Like, comment, and engage with posts from students nationwide.

College Confessions

Share anonymous text-based confessions visible only to students from your college. A safe space for honest conversations.

Verified & Safe

Every user is verified with their college ID card. Our team manually reviews profiles to ensure a safe, authentic community of real students.

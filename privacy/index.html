SNEK ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, college details, profile picture.
  • User Content: Posts, messages, groups, confessions, chats, calls, and other content you share.
  • Communication Data: Metadata related to audio calls, video calls, and chat (e.g., call duration, time, participants).
  • Important: We do not record or store the actual content of your audio/video calls or private chats.
  • Usage Data: App activity, preferences, interactions, and log data.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, crash reports.
  • Location Data (Optional): If you enable location-based features (e.g., finding matches nearby).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve dating, networking, messaging, audio, and video calling features.
  • Facilitate safe communication between users.
  • Detect spam, abuse, or misuse of chat/call features.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Ensure app performance and security.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With other users (based on your privacy settings).
  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, push notifications, real-time communication infrastructure).
  • For legal compliance or to protect our rights.

Your Privacy Matters: We prioritize the security of your data, especially your private communications. We never record or store the content of your audio/video calls or private chats.

4. Your Choices

  • Update or delete your profile anytime.
  • Manage permissions like microphone, camera, and notifications in your device settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at [support email].

5. Security

We use encryption and reasonable safeguards to protect your communication data. However, no system is 100% secure.

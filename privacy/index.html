Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 Sep 2025

1. Introduction

SNEK is dedicated to safeguarding your personal information and upholding your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy delineates the types of information we collect, the purposes for its collection, and the measures we undertake to protect it. By utilizing the SNEK application, you expressly consent to the data practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

To provide and enhance our services, we collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account and Profile Information: This includes, but is not limited to, your full name, email address, phone number, stated gender, and academic institution details.
  • Identity Verification Data: To ensure the integrity of our community, we require the submission of a photographic image of your official college identification document alongside a contemporaneous selfie for verification purposes.
  • User-Generated Content: This encompasses all media you upload, including profile photographs, images posted within the application sourced from your device's gallery or camera, textual posts, group contributions, and confessions.
  • Communication Data: All content generated through the use of our messaging, audio calling, and video calling features is processed. We explicitly do not record or store the content of audio or video calls; however, metadata associated with such communications (including duration, timestamps, and participant identifiers) may be retained for operational purposes.
  • Technical and Device Information: We automatically collect specific data related to your device and interaction with our services. This includes your IP address, device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic information such as application crash reports.
  • Push Notification Tokens: We collect a unique device token to facilitate the delivery of push notifications to your device.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

It is expressly stated that SNEK does not collect, track, or process your precise or approximate geolocation data.

4. Purposes of Data Processing

Your information is utilized for the following legitimate business interests:

  • User Verification: To authenticate your status as a bona fide college student, thereby maintaining a secure and trustworthy platform environment.
  • Service Provisioning: To facilitate the core functionalities of the application, including dating features, social networking, direct messaging, and audio/video communication.
  • Service Personalization: To curate and tailor your individual experience, including your user profile and content feed.
  • Communication: To transmit operational and promotional push notifications regarding matches, messages, application updates, and special offers.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To monitor for and mitigate instances of fraudulent activity, impersonation, and other forms of platform abuse.

5. Application Permissions

The SNEK application requests the following permissions on your device, which are integral to its functionality:

  • Camera: Permission is required to capture images and video for identity verification selfies and for participation in video calls.
  • Gallery/Storage: Permission is required to access and upload photographs from your device's local storage for use as profile pictures or within posts.
  • Microphone: Permission is required to capture audio for participation in audio and video calls.
  • Notifications: Permission is required to display alerts and push notifications on your device.

Your Privacy Matters: We prioritize the security of your data, especially your private communications. We never record or store the content of your audio/video calls or private chats.

6. Account Deactivation and Data Deletion

  • Profile Deactivation: You retain the right to deactivate your user profile at any time directly through the application's interface. This action will render your profile temporarily inactive.
  • Account Deletion: To initiate the permanent and irreversible deletion of your account and all associated personal data, you must submit a formal request via email to our administrative team at [support@snekapp.com]. Processing will commence upon receipt of your request.

7. Data Security Protocols

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Particularly sensitive data, such as college identification documents and verification photographs, is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols.

8. Policy Regarding Minors

SNEK is strictly prohibited for use by individuals under the age of eighteen (18). We do not knowingly solicit or collect any information from minors.

9. Amendments to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy periodically. Any such amendments will be communicated to users through in-application notifications or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the commencement of this policy will be revised accordingly.
